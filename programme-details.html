Programme Details

📣 Dear Parents & Teachers

Welcome to the Daily Telugu Practice WhatsApp Group. This group is created to help students improve their Telugu vocabulary with just 20 minutes of daily effort—simple and stress-free!

📅 Your Daily Routine:

📩 Receive a message with 40 Telugu words.
✍️ Make your child write the dictation.
✅ Evaluate their writing.
📤 Share the result in the group.
📝 Update the score in the shared Google Sheet.

📚 Programme Overview:

🔢 18 Stages, covering 3000+ words.
📘 Each stage includes 200 words and 50 worksheets.
📝 Each worksheet has 40 randomly selected words.
📈 Students can attempt 2 to 3 tests daily.
⏳ Completion time: 6 months to 2 years (based on student pace).

🎯 Final Goal:

✔️ Be able to read and write Telugu up to Grade 10 level.
✔️ Be well-prepared for any board exams in Telugu.

📊 Weekly Progress Reports (for subscribed members only)

A report showing your child’s progress will be shared privately with you on WhatsApp every week.

🌟 Key Takeaways:

📖 Improved Reading Skills – Daily exposure enhances vocabulary & comprehension.
✍️ Enhanced Writing Abilities – Regular practice refines handwriting & expression.
👂 Better Listening Skills – Dictations train attention & auditory processing.
🧠 Increased Concentration – Focused tasks build mental discipline.
⏱️ Self-Paced Learning – Children learn at their own comfort level.
🏆 Healthy Competition – Progress tracking inspires steady improvement.

🙌 Final Note:

With consistent daily practice, your child will see noticeable improvement in reading, writing, and confidence in Telugu.

Wishing you joyful and successful learning!
